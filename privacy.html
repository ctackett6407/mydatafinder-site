Privacy Policy – MyDataFinder

MyDataFinder is a personal tool used by the owner of this app to export and analyze their own TikTok account data.

The app uses TikTok’s official OAuth to access the user’s account after explicit consent. Data retrieved is stored locally on the user’s device and is not shared or sold.

Data types may include profile information and public video metrics as permitted by TikTok scopes (user.info.*, video.list). No payment data is collected.
